# InterviewIQ AI Brand Guidelines

## Brand Name

**InterviewIQ AI**

Always written as **InterviewIQ AI**.

Never alter capitalization or spacing.

### Acceptable Usage

- InterviewIQ AI
- INTERVIEWIQ AI
- InterviewIQ

### Unacceptable Usage

- interviewiq ai
- Interview IQ
- Interviewiq AI
- Interview-IQ
- Interview IQ AI

---

# Brand Personality

InterviewIQ AI is an intelligent interview coach that helps users prepare for real-world interviews through AI-powered analysis and personalized feedback.

The brand should communicate:

- Intelligence
- Professionalism
- Confidence
- Innovation
- Trust
- Growth

Avoid looking playful or overly corporate.

---

# Logo Usage

## Clear Space

Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "I" in the logo around all sides.

---

## Minimum Size

Digital

- Width: 140px

Print

- Width: 30mm

---

## Logo Don'ts

Do not

- Stretch
- Rotate
- Change colors
- Add shadows
- Add gradients
- Place on busy backgrounds
- Use low-resolution logos

# Overall Product Goal

InterviewIQ AI should feel like a premium SaaS platform that helps candidates improve their interview performance through AI-driven insights, resume analysis, real-time mock interviews, and personalized feedback.

Every screen should communicate clarity, confidence, and professionalism while remaining approachable and easy to use.
